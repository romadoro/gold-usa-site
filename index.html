Who We Are

Company Information

Gold USA Inc. is a U.S.-based company focused on precious metals, jewelry, sourcing, and brand development. Our work is centered on quality, presentation, and long-term value within the gold and jewelry industry.

What We Do

Gold USA Inc. supports activities related to precious metals and jewelry, including sourcing, distribution, and brand-focused business development. We work with partners across the industry to deliver consistent, high-quality results.

Our Focus

We are committed to building a professional foundation around quality, reliability, and a clear long-term vision in the jewelry and gold sector. Every engagement is approached with care and precision.

Our Mission

"Building long-term value through integrity, craftsmanship, and a deep understanding of the precious metals and jewelry market."

Founded in 1998 and based in the United States, Gold USA Inc. has served clients seeking a trusted partner in the gold and jewelry industry for over 25 years — from sourcing to brand development.

What We Offer

Our Services

From sourcing to brand development, we provide end-to-end support for businesses operating in the precious metals and jewelry space.

Precious Metal Sourcing

We identify and secure high-quality gold, silver, and other precious metals through established sourcing networks in the U.S. and internationally.

Distribution

Reliable and efficient distribution of jewelry and metal products to retailers, wholesalers, and private clients with an emphasis on quality control at every step.

Brand Development

Strategic guidance for jewelry and gold brands looking to establish or grow their presence — from identity development to market positioning.

Business Consulting

Industry expertise available to businesses navigating the complexities of the precious metals market, from compliance to growth strategy.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality standards applied across every product and transaction, ensuring every piece meets the expectations of our clients and partners.
